FancyGrid provides comprehensive collection of grid and form solutions for Enterprise.  
We try to do elegant samples for real business tasks with focus on DataViz.

#### Features:

* Paging
* Sorting
* Filtering
* Validation
* Ajax data
* Files data
* RESTful
* flexible CRUD
* Integration with charts
* Form module 
* Touch Support
